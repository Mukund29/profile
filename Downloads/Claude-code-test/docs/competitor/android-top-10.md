### FinTrack Android Whitespace Opportunity
> Android is FinTrack's **biggest differentiator**: SMS parsing is Android-exclusive and none of the global competitors (YNAB, PocketGuard, EveryDollar) have it. Indian competitors (Moneyview, Axio) have SMS parsing but no UK/US support and no 50-30-20 discipline framework. **FinTrack uniquely combines SMS parsing + Setu AA + Plaid + TrueLayer + 50-30-20 discipline scoring across 3 markets.**
